City Life Church Content and Engagement Plan

Facebook Page, Instagram, and Facebook Group Content Strategy

  • Regular Posts: Post daily updates, inspirational quotes, and event reminders on Facebook and Instagram.
  • Live Sessions: Host weekly live sessions discussing biblical topics, community events, and Q&A sessions.
  • Interactive Content: Create polls, quizzes, and surveys to engage followers and gather feedback.
  • Video Content: Share testimonies, sermon snippets, and behind-the-scenes videos to provide a personal touch.
  • User-Generated Content: Encourage members to share their stories, photos, and experiences related to church activities.
  • Event Promotion: Promote upcoming events, workshops, and community outreach programs regularly.
  • Community Spotlights: Highlight volunteer efforts, success stories, and member achievements to foster community spirit.
  • Seasonal Themes: Align content with holidays, seasons, and church milestones to keep the feed fresh and relevant.
  • Educational Content: Share informative articles, infographics, and educational resources on faith-related topics.
  • Engagement Initiatives: Initiate challenges, group activities, and shoutouts to keep followers actively participating.

Engagement Funnel

Awareness:

  • Create engaging posts about City Life Church's culture, events, and community on the main Facebook page.
  • Use paid ads to reach a wider audience and promote the Facebook Group and church events.

Interest:

  • Offer a valuable digital freebie to encourage people to join the Facebook Group.
  • Run targeted giveaways to generate interest and increase group membership.

Engagement:

  • Host virtual and offline events to engage group members and foster a sense of community.
  • Regularly post interactive content, such as polls, quizzes, and challenges, to keep the group active.
  • Send 50 DMs a day to followers, inviting them to join the Facebook Group and participate in upcoming events.
  • Follow the script:
    • Follow up with a picture
    • Invite to church
    • Comment on people’s posts
    • “Hey! Thank you for the engagement, check your DMs”
    • “Hey! Thank you for the engagement, I have a present for you, check your DMs”

Conversion:

  • Use Facebook Live to interact with members in real-time and provide valuable spiritual content.
  • Develop nurturing email campaigns to convert group members into active church attendees.

Retention:

  • 1-5% will accept invite to church (20-100 invites accepted)
  • 70-90% show up (14-90 show up)
  • Keep attending by: 3-45 regular attendees

Email Campaigns

Internal Campaign to Engage Leadership

  • Send an email to non-attending and non-engaged members, encouraging them to participate in church activities and leadership roles.
  • Highlight the benefits of active engagement and leadership, and provide a clear call-to-action to get involved.
  • Share testimonials from current leaders and engaged members about their positive experiences.

Outbound Campaign

  • Send an email to potential new members, introducing them to City Life Church and inviting them to join the Facebook Group.
  • Include information about upcoming events, community activities, and the church's mission and values.
  • Provide a clear call-to-action to join the group and attend an event.

Internal Campaign to Introduce New Staff

  • Send an email introducing the church's new staff members to the congregation.
  • Include a brief bio and photo of each new staff member, highlighting their background and role at the church.
  • Encourage members to welcome the new staff and get to know them better.

C. Organic Content Creation:

  • One-Minute Blurbs: Create short, impactful videos answering common questions and providing spiritual insights. Share these videos in the group and on the main Facebook page to attract new members.
  • Content Repurposing: Turn high-engagement organic content into targeted paid ads to reach a broader audience. Use this strategy to drive traffic to the group and promote upcoming events.

Content Ideas for Most Asked Biblical Questions

  • What is the Bible? – Create an introductory video explaining what the Bible is and its significance in Christianity.
  • Who wrote the Bible? – Develop an article detailing the authors of the Bible and the historical context of its writing.
  • How should I read the Bible? – Offer a reading plan and tips for beginners on how to approach reading the Bible.
  • What is the main message of the Bible? – Produce a video summarizing the overarching narrative and message of the Bible.
  • What are the different versions of the Bible? – Write a blog post comparing various Bible translations and their unique features.
  • How do I interpret difficult passages in the Bible? – Host a webinar on hermeneutics and the principles of biblical interpretation.
  • What is the Old Testament? – Create an infographic explaining the structure and content of the Old Testament.
  • What is the New Testament? – Develop a podcast series exploring the books and themes of the New Testament.
  • What are the Ten Commandments? – Share a video series breaking down each of the Ten Commandments and their relevance today.
  • What are the Gospels? – Produce an animated video explaining the four Gospels and their unique perspectives on Jesus' life.

Content Ideas for Most Asked Questions About the Existence of God

  • If there is a God, then why does evil exist? – Host a panel discussion on the problem of evil and the Christian perspective.
  • If God is loving, why do bad things happen? – Write an article addressing theodicy and the balance of divine love and human suffering.
  • If God created everything, who created God? – Produce a video explaining the concept of God's existence outside of time and space.
  • If God knows everything, do we still have free will? – Create a blog post discussing divine omniscience and human freedom.
  • If God is real, why can't we see Him? – Host a live Q&A session addressing the nature of God's invisibility and spiritual presence.
  • If God answers prayers, why do some prayers go unanswered? – Develop a series exploring the dynamics of prayer and divine intervention.
  • If God is just, why are there injustices in the world? – Produce a podcast episode discussing justice, human responsibility, and divine sovereignty.
  • If God wants us to be happy, why do we suffer? – Write an eBook exploring the relationship between happiness, suffering, and spiritual growth.
  • If God is omnipotent, why doesn't He prevent natural disasters? – Create an infographic on divine omnipotence and the nature of natural disasters.
  • If God loves everyone, why are there different religions? – Host a webinar on religious pluralism and the Christian view of salvation.

Content Ideas for Most Asked Questions About Godly Marriage

  • What does the Bible say about marriage? – Develop a comprehensive guide outlining biblical principles for marriage and how to apply them.
  • How can I build a godly marriage? – Create a video series offering practical advice and spiritual insights on building a strong, faith-centered marriage.
  • How do I handle conflicts in a Christian marriage? – Host a workshop or webinar on conflict resolution and communication skills for couples.
  • What is the role of a husband and wife in a Christian marriage? – Write an article exploring the biblical roles of husband and wife and how they complement each other.
  • How can we keep God at the center of our marriage? – Share testimonies and stories from couples who have successfully made God the center of their relationship.
  • What does it mean to submit to one another in marriage? – Produce a podcast episode discussing mutual submission and respect in Christian marriages.
  • How can we prioritize intimacy in a godly marriage? – Create an eBook on fostering emotional, spiritual, and physical intimacy in marriage.
  • How can we strengthen our marriage through prayer? – Develop a prayer guide for couples, focusing on strengthening their spiritual bond.
  • How do we navigate cultural challenges in marriage? – Host a panel discussion on cultural diversity and its impact on Christian marriages.
  • What are practical ways to support each other spiritually in marriage? – Create a series of blog posts offering practical tips for spiritual support and growth as a couple.
